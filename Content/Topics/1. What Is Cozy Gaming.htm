What is Cozy Gaming?

Definition and Characteristics

Cozy gaming refers to a genre of video games that emphasize relaxation, comfort, and a sense of warmth. These games typically feature:

  • soothing visuals,

  • gentle soundtracks, and

  • low-stress gameplay mechanics.

Unlike more intense genres that focus on competition or survival, cozy games are designed to create a calming and enjoyable experience. Characteristics of cozy gaming include simple controls, non-violent themes, and an emphasis on exploration, creativity, and storytelling.

The primary aim of this project is to popularize the concept of cozy gaming, bringing it into the spotlight for a broader audience. Additionally, the project aims to present and highlight some of the most popular cozy gaming titles, offering recommendations and insights that cater to both seasoned gamers and newcomers alike. Through this comprehensive overview, we aspire to create an appreciation for the unique charm and appeal of cozy gaming, ultimately enriching the gaming experiences of many.
